Challenge 1: The Basics

Goal: Understand how distance affects gravity

1. Place TWO objects (any size) on the canvas

2. Watch the green force arrows between them

3. Drag one object CLOSER to the other

4. Drag it FARTHER away

Question: What happens to the arrows when objects are closer? Record your observation in your worksheet!

Challenge 2: Mass Matters

Goal: See how mass affects gravity

1. Clear all objects

2. Place a STAR (large mass)

3. Place a SMALL PLANET near it

4. Watch the force arrows - which are larger?

5. Press Play and watch what happens!

Question: Does the planet orbit the star, or does the star orbit the planet? Why?

Challenge 3: Create a Solar System

Goal: Make planets orbit a star

1. Place ONE STAR in the center

2. Add 3-4 SMALL or MEDIUM planets around it

3. Press Play

4. Observe which planet moves fastest

Question: Does the closest planet move fastest or slowest? Why?

Hint: If planets fly away, try placing them closer to the star!

Challenge 4: Everything Pulls Everything

Goal: Discover universal gravitation

1. Create a system with 5+ objects

2. Mix different sizes

3. Watch ALL the force arrows

4. Notice: EVERY object pulls on EVERY other object!

Question: Count how many force arrows you see. If you have 5 objects, how many pairs are pulling on each other?

Hint: With 5 objects, there should be 10 arrows (5√ó4√∑2)
